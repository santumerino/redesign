Games

I always wanted to make my own videogames, but I only truly got started in 2017. Most of them were made for :game jams, and as such they're completely gratis. You can play them here!

My first ever long-term game project was YARG, released on September 16th 2022. It's a rage platformer game featuring a cheerful AI that wants you to succeed! You can check it out on Steam or on Itch.io!
